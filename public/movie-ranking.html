The Best Movies According to Christina

My top 5 movies of all-time.

50 First Dates

One of the best rom coms out there. Very simple and funny.

The Usual Suspects

Very well made crime movie. Unexpected ending

Bhaagmati

South-Indian horror-thriller movie. The ending is surprising

Ponyo

Very calm and soothing. Great to watch while chilling.

Golmaal

Best comedy bollywood film series ever. Too many iconic jokes.
